Bali


Bali is one of the most beautiful islands in Indonesia. It is famous for its stunning beaches, rich culture, ancient temples, and scenic landscapes. It is a perfect place for both adventure and relaxation.

  1. Uluwatu Temple - known for its beautiful sunsets over the ocean.
  2. Tegallalang Rice Terraces - experience the lush green fields and stunning views.
  3. Mount Batur - enjoy an early morning sunrise trek on an active volcano.
  4. Ubud Monkey Forest - home to playful monkeys and ancient temples.
  5. Seminyak Beach - famous for its vibrant nightlife and luxury resorts.
  • Nasi Goreng - traditional Indonesian fried rice served with egg.
  • Babi Guling - Balinese roasted pork with local spices.
  • Satay - skewered meat with peanut sauce.
  • Lawar - mixture of vegetables, coconut, and minced meat.
  • Balinese Coffee - famous for its strong aroma and rich taste.
